Reporting

TransAM contains a wide variety of reports on asset inventory, capital projects, and more. Reports can be exported as CSV files or PDF files that can easily be printed. Reports currently available include:

  • Inventory Reports
    • Asset Age Report
    • Asset Funding Report
  • Capital Needs Reports
    • Unconstrained Capital Projects Report
    • Needs Versus Funding Statewide Report
    • ALI Funding Report
    • Capital Plan Report
    • Bond Request Report
  • Planning Reports
    • Vehicle Replacement Report
    • State of Good Repair Report
    • Disposition Report
