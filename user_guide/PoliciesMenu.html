Policies Menu

Overview ›› Main Menu ››

This menu lets you access functionality related to policy, capital planning, and audits. The Policies link lets you manage policies that define the useful life and asset replacement rules in effect for your organizations. The second part of the menu contains tools and utilities that relate to capital planning (Capital Projects, Project Planner, and SOGR Capital Project Analyzer). Capital project interfaces let you generate, view, and schedule new projects based on your policy. The final part of the menu consists of links to audit tables (All Audit Results, Annual Inventory Update Results), which show lists of assets that require work to complete the given audit.
