Glossary

Actions Menu: A menu that offers specific actions you can take that relate to what you are seeing on the TransAM screen. In most deployments, the Action menu is labeled, "Actions..."


User Menu: A menu that appears under your own name in the upper-right corner of the TransAM screen. This menu lets you access your user settings, messages, tasks, and other items, depending upon your role and responsibilities.
