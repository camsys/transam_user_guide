Activity Line Item (ALI)

Capital Planning ››

ALIs are the building blocks of a project. A project can range in complexity. A simple project may have one line item (the purchase of five single vans) while a complex project has many line items (Purchase seven 35 FT Buses, Purchase two Vans, Purchase four 40 FT Buses, etc.).


ALIs are created for SOGR and new capital projects differently:

  • Since a SOGR project maintains your existing inventory, the SOGR Project Analyzer automatically creates ALIs when policy rules designate an asset as needing replacement.
  • Since a new capital project provides new or expanded services, you will build the project by manually creating ALIs for the different components of your project.
