Vehicle Replacement Report

Reporting ›› Canned Reports ›› Planning Reports ››

This report finds and displays summary data for all revenue vehicles that are scheduled to be replaced in a user-defined fiscal year.


The report groups data by agency and fiscal year with each row in the report displaying a Fiscal Year, Type, Sub Type, Count, Book Value, and Replacement Cost. I short, the report displays replacement information (asset count, asset book value, and asset replacement cost) for the different revenue vehicle subtypes.
