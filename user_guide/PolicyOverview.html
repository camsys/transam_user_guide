To navigate to a policy

  1. Choose Policies (Main Menu) -> Policies to see a list of your policies.
  2. Choose the policy you want to work with from the list.


TransAM will direct you to the policy detail page. Each Policy has three levels:

  • Agency Level
  • Asset Type Rules
  • Subtype Rules
