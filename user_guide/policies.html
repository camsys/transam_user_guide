Policy

A TransAM Policy is a set of rules and parameters that determine how TransAM schedules and estimates the cost to replace assets. While an agency can create and modify multiple policies, the agency can only have one current policy at a time. TransAM applies the given policy to an inventory on an asset by asset basis so that replacement information is reflected on every individual asset.
